Orchid (Phalaenopsis)

Orchids are stunning, exotic plants known for their elegant flowers that come in various colors and patterns. Phalaenopsis orchids are especially popular due to their beauty and ease of care.

Plant Description

Phalaenopsis orchids are epiphytic plants native to Southeast Asia. They are known for their long-lasting, vibrant flowers that come in many shades, including pink, white, purple, and yellow.

Common Names

  • Moth Orchid
  • Phalaenopsis Orchid

Plant Care Tips

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Watering: Water once a week, allowing the roots to dry between waterings. Use lukewarm water.
  • Soil: Use a well-draining orchid mix or bark.

Fun Facts

Orchids are one of the largest plant families, with over 25,000 species. The name "orchid" comes from the Greek word for testicle, referring to the shape of the plant's tubers.
