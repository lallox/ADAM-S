Haworthia Fasciata

The Haworthia fasciata, also known as the **Zebra Plant**, is a small, rosette-forming succulent plant that is a popular houseplant.

Plant Description

The Haworthia fasciata is known for its striking green and white striped leaves that form a beautiful rosette. It's an easy-to-care-for succulent, perfect for both beginners and experienced gardeners.

Common Names

  • Zebra Plant
  • Pearl Plant
  • African Haworthia

Plant Care Tips

  • Light: Bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight which may burn the leaves.
  • Watering: Water sparingly. Allow the soil to dry out completely between waterings to avoid root rot.
  • Soil: Well-draining cactus or succulent soil mix.
  • Temperature: Prefers temperatures between 65-85°F (18-29°C). Protect from frost.

Fun Facts

The Haworthia fasciata is a great air purifier, making it an excellent plant for improving indoor air quality. It can also tolerate some neglect, making it perfect for busy plant parents!
