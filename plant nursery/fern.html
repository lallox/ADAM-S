Fern

Ferns are a classic plant, perfect for creating a lush, tropical atmosphere in your home. They prefer shady spots and high humidity.

Care Tips:

  • Keep the soil moist, but not soggy.
  • Place in indirect light.
  • Ferns thrive in humid conditions, so mist regularly.
