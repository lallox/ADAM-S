Coleus

Coleus is a colorful and easy-to-grow plant with bold foliage. It adds vibrant color to any space with minimal care.

Care Tips:

  • Prefers bright, indirect light.
  • Water when the top of the soil feels dry.
  • Prune regularly to keep the plant bushy.
